Media queries in CSS3 extended the CSS2 media types idea: Instead of looking for a type of device, they look at the capability of the device.Media queries can be used to check many things, such as:width and height of the viewport orientation of the viewport (landscape or portrait)resolution Using media queries are a popular technique for delivering a tailored style sheet to desktops, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones (such as iPhone and Android phones)
